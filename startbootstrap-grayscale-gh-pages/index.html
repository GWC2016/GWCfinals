Our Inspiration

Our group is trying to raise cultural awareness about countries around the world. It is very important for people to be aware of the world around them. Having mindfulness can help people enjoy the world around them more, and to understand themselves more. Understanding different cultures from various countries can help people be more accepting of those around them. Cultural awareness can help build relationships in more diverse environments.

Our Project

"A people without the knowledge of their past history, origin and culture is like a tree without roots." -Marcus Garvey. We wanted to make a web game that teaches people about the different cultures that make up our world today. Our project is aimed towards the younger generation and targeting people who might not have experience visiting foreign country. For our project, We were thinking about doing a side scroller type of game, like Mario. We wanted to make it so that the player travels through different countries and catches facts of information about that specific country. Each level results in one specific country with three or more facts to catch.
